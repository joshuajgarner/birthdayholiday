Night 01 · Girona
Our first night
A rooftop, a cathedral, a cocktail

Hotel Ultonia, Girona — in the heart of the old town since 1962, a few minutes' walk from the cathedral steps and the painted houses along the river. Its rooftop cocktail terrace looks straight across the rooftops to the cathedral, lit up gold after dark. That's where I'm taking you the moment we've dropped the bags.

One night in a city built for wandering — cobbles, candlelight, and you. I can't think of a better way to start.
